What we do

Virtual Reality

The process is very simple in principle. The patient puts on VR glasses and is shown scenes via an app that place him more and more in a situation that seems threatening to him. Scenes can be, for example, an elevator, an airplane or a subway, which is increasingly being filled with passengers. The patient decides for himself how far he wants to go. In the worst case, a panic attack cannot be ruled out, but there is always the possibility of tearing your glasses off your head.

Our Therapy

The Invirto app is a smartphone application for Android and iOS and the digital image of psychotherapy for anxiety disorders. This is exactly why the Invirto app is structured like a classic therapy as a course with different goals and content. You complete course units one after the other, which teach you the most effective content from various experts for the treatment of anxiety disorders. Invirto provides you with content in the form of a course with units that build on each other. In the app, just like in normal psychotherapy, you will find lessons in which, like in a therapy session, information is conveyed and certain aspects of fear are reflected on. Finally, you can experiment with strategies against fear. Just like you would do in classic therapy.

Who are we?

Let's introduce our selves

Fear is a natural basic feeling and in extreme cases can be life sustaining. It becomes a problem when it becomes pathological. Millions of people suffer from anxiety disorders, many lack the possibility of therapy.

The startup Sympatient shows a new way out of the disease and relies on virtual realities.Our team is highly motivated and unites a wide range of backgrounds. We combine expertise in psychology, computer science, business, 3D design and medtech to solve our challenges. This results in an interdisciplinary approach to problem solving, through which we aim to account for all stakeholders of the healthcare system.
